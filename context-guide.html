Definition: The art of crafting clear, precise instructions to guide AI outputs effectively.

  • Example: "Write a motivational quote."
  • Detailed Example: "You are a leadership coach. Write a motivational quote for startup founders, use a friendly tone."

2Ô∏è‚É£ What is Context Engineering?

Definition: Setting up the environment, memory, tools, and system roles that shape how AI processes prompts.

  • Includes AI role, tool access, prior chat, retrieved documents.
  • Ensures outputs are consistent and contextually accurate.

3Ô∏è‚É£ Why Both Matter Together

  • Prompt alone: Good for simple, one-time queries.
  • Context Engineering: Essential for long tasks, multi-step workflows, reliable automation.
